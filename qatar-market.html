Key Insight: Charitable Focus

Qatar visitors show a unique combination of interest in both the social aspects of Ramadan (Iftar meals: 100%) and the charitable aspects (Zakat: 89%). This creates an opportunity to position Abu Dhabi as a destination where visitors can engage in meaningful charitable activities while enjoying authentic social experiences during Ramadan.

Strategic Recommendations

1. Positioning Strategy: "Charitable Spirit of Ramadan"

Position Abu Dhabi as a destination that embodies the charitable spirit of Ramadan, offering visitors opportunities to participate in meaningful giving while enjoying authentic social experiences. Emphasize the combination of charitable activities and community engagement.

2. Experience Development

Create experiences that combine charitable giving with social engagement:

  • Charity Iftars: Special Iftar events where a portion of proceeds goes to charity
  • Volunteer Opportunities: Organized volunteer activities for visitors during Ramadan
  • Charitable Tours: Guided tours of charitable initiatives in Abu Dhabi
  • Giving Packages: Special travel packages that include charitable donations

3. Cause-Related Marketing

Develop marketing initiatives that connect travel to charitable causes:

  • Donation Matching: Match visitor donations to local charities during Ramadan
  • Impact Reporting: Provide visitors with reports on the impact of their charitable contributions
  • Charity Partnerships: Form partnerships with respected Qatari charitable organizations
  • Social Proof: Showcase testimonials from previous visitors about their charitable experiences

Proposed Campaign: "Ramadan of Giving"

Launch a campaign that positions Abu Dhabi as a destination where visitors can experience the true charitable spirit of Ramadan. The campaign would highlight the various ways visitors can give back while enjoying authentic Ramadan experiences in Abu Dhabi.

Key Campaign Elements:

  • Video series showcasing the impact of charitable initiatives during Ramadan
  • Social media campaign with #RamadanOfGiving hashtag
  • Partnerships with Qatari charitable organizations and influencers
  • Special "Giving Packages" that include charitable donations with bookings

Implementation Timeline

  • Q3 2025 (July-September): Charitable partnership development and experience design
  • Q4 2025 (October-December): Content creation and giving package development
  • Q1 2026 (Mid-January): Campaign launch in Qatar (30 days before Ramadan)
  • Q1 2026 (Late January-February): Charitable organization partnerships activation
  • Ramadan 2026 (February 17 - March 21): Charitable experience delivery and impact reporting
