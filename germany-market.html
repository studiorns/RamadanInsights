Key Insight: Practical Information Needs

German travelers show the highest concern for practical and logistical aspects of traveling during Ramadan, with specific concerns about restaurant availability (29%), transportation schedules (31%), and daily routines (35%). Unlike other markets that focus on cultural sensitivity or visual experiences, German visitors prioritize clear, detailed information and structured experiences.

Strategic Recommendations

1. Positioning Strategy: "Practical Cultural Immersion"

Position Abu Dhabi as a well-organized destination with clear information for travelers during Ramadan. Emphasize the structured nature of Ramadan experiences and the availability of detailed practical information to address German concerns.

2. Experience Development

Create structured experiences with clear practical information:

  • Educational Workshops: Structured learning experiences about Ramadan basics
  • Guided Cultural Experiences: Tours with detailed itineraries and clear expectations
  • Practical Experience Packages: Bundled experiences with all logistics handled
  • Scheduled Dining Options: Clear information about restaurant availability and hours

3. Content Strategy

Develop German-language content that emphasizes practical information:

  • Practical Guides: Detailed information about daily schedules during Ramadan
  • Transportation Maps: Clear information about transportation options and schedules
  • Restaurant Directories: Comprehensive listings of dining options with hours
  • FAQ Resources: Detailed answers to common practical questions

Proposed Campaign: "Ramadan: Organized & Authentic"

Launch a campaign that positions Ramadan in Abu Dhabi as a well-organized cultural experience with all the practical information German travelers need. The campaign would emphasize the structured nature of the experiences and the availability of detailed information.

Key Campaign Elements:

  • Comprehensive digital guide with detailed practical information
  • Social media campaign highlighting organized experiences with clear expectations
  • Partnerships with German travel agencies specializing in structured cultural tours
  • Dedicated German-language support line for practical questions

Implementation Timeline

  • Q3 2025 (July-September): Practical guide development and experience structuring
  • Q4 2025 (October-November): Content creation and asset development
  • Q4 2025 (Mid-December): Campaign launch in Germany (62 days before Ramadan)
  • Q1 2026 (January-February): German-language support system development
  • Ramadan 2026 (February 17 - March 21): Structured experience delivery with practical support
