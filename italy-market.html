Key Insight: Culinary Focus

Italian travelers show the strongest interest in food experiences (53%) among all markets, significantly higher than their interest in other aspects of Ramadan. This culinary focus, combined with lower cultural sensitivity concerns (28% vs. 51% for UK), creates an opportunity to position Abu Dhabi as a gastronomic destination during Ramadan.

Strategic Recommendations

1. Positioning Strategy: "Culinary Cultural Journey"

Position Abu Dhabi as a gastronomic destination during Ramadan, highlighting the unique culinary traditions and experiences available. Emphasize the fusion of traditional Ramadan cuisine with contemporary culinary approaches that would appeal to Italian food enthusiasts.

2. Experience Development

Create food-centered experiences that appeal to Italian travelers:

  • Ramadan Culinary Workshops: Hands-on cooking classes featuring Ramadan specialties
  • Chef Collaborations: Special events featuring Italian and Emirati chefs creating fusion dishes
  • Food Tours: Guided tours of markets and food establishments during Ramadan
  • Gourmet Iftar Experiences: Premium dining experiences highlighting the gastronomic aspects of Iftar

3. Content Strategy

Develop Italian-language content that emphasizes the culinary aspects of Ramadan:

  • "Taste of Ramadan" Guide: Detailed information about Ramadan cuisine and dining options
  • Chef Profiles: Feature stories about Emirati chefs and their Ramadan specialties
  • Recipe Collections: Shareable content featuring Ramadan recipes adapted for Italian audiences
  • Food Influencer Partnerships: Collaborate with Italian food bloggers and influencers

Proposed Campaign: "Sapori di Ramadan" (Flavors of Ramadan)

Launch a campaign that positions Ramadan in Abu Dhabi as a unique culinary festival that Italian food enthusiasts shouldn't miss. The campaign would highlight the diverse flavors, ingredients, and dining experiences available during Ramadan.

Key Campaign Elements:

  • Video series featuring Italian chefs exploring Ramadan cuisine in Abu Dhabi
  • Social media campaign with #SaporiDiRamadan hashtag
  • Partnerships with Italian culinary magazines and food festivals
  • Dedicated Italian-language microsite with recipes, chef interviews, and booking options

Implementation Timeline

  • Q3 2025 (July-September): Chef partnerships development and culinary experience design
  • Q4 2025 (October-November): Content creation and asset development
  • Q4 2025 (Early December): Campaign launch in Italy (74 days before Ramadan)
  • Q1 2026 (January-February): Food influencer trips and content amplification
  • Ramadan 2026 (February 17 - March 21): Culinary experience delivery and real-time optimization
